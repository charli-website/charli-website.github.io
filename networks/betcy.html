Betcy Jose-Thota is a doctoral student at the Graduate School of Public and International Affairs at the University of Pittsburgh, focusing on Foreign and Security Policy and International Development. She holds a B.A. from the University of Texas at Austin and a J.D. from the University of Houston Law Center.  Her research interests are in armed conflict, international humanitarian law, international norms and human rights.
